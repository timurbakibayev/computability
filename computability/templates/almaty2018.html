Workshop on Computability Problems

August 2 - 9, 2018, Almaty, Kazakhstan

The Workshop on Computability Problems is organized and hosted by Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics at Al-Farabi Kazakh National University. It will be held at the University from August 2 to August 9, 2018.

The program of the Workshop consists of 50-minutes plenary talks and research in groups. Participants: there are 9 invited speakers more than 20 researches from Kazakhstan, and students.

It is expected that the main outcome of the Wokshop will be a list of open problems suggested by the speakers.

Submissions

Abstracts of plenary talks must be submitted as tex-files.

The deadline for the abstract submission is July 15, 2018. Files with problems may be submitted after discussion during the workshop.

Location and venue

The conference will take place in Almaty at Mechanics and Mathematics Faculty, in the campus of Al-Farabi Kazakh National University. More details and maps

Accommodation

The invited speakers will be hosted at the Astana International Hotel and completely supported by the organizers of the Workshop.

Registration

Registration started just half an hour before the openning of the Workshop. The students are free of the registration fee. For all others the registration fee is 20000 Tenge (approx. 50 Euros). It includes coffee breaks, transportation, support of the workshop domain, and workshop materials.

Contacts

The programme chair is academician Yuri L. Ershov.

The chair of the Organizing Committee is Serikzhan Badaev.

Enquiries on scientific matters should be sent to the members of the Organizing Committee Timur Bakibayev or Kuanysh Abeshev.
