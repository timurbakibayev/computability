Workshop on Computability Problems

August 2 - 9, 2018, Almaty, Kazakhstan

The Workshop on Computability Problems is organized and hosted by Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics at Al-Farabi Kazakh National University. It will be held at the Al-Farabi Kazakh National University from August 2 to August 9, 2018.

The program of the Workshop consists of 50-minutes plenary talks and research in groups. There are 9 invited speakers, researchers, and students from Kazakhstan.
