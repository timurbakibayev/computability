Workshop on Computability Problems

August 2 - 9, 2018, Almaty, Kazakhstan

The Workshop on Computability Problems is organized and hosted by Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics at Al-Farabi Kazakh National University. It will be held at the University from August 2 to August 9, 2018.

The program of the Workshop consists of 50-minutes plenary talks and research in groups. There are 9 invited speakers, researchers from Kazakhstan, and students.

Submissions

Abstracts of plenary talks must be submitted as tex-files.

The deadline for abstract submission is July 15, 2018. Files with problems may be submitted during the workshop.

Accommodation

The invited speakers will be hosted at the Astana International Hotel (ссылка) and completely supported by the organizers of the Workshop.

Registration

Registration started just half an hour before the openning of the Workshop. The students are free of the registration fee. For all others the registration fee is 20000 Tenge (approx. 50 Euros). It includes coffee breaks, transportation, support of the domain, and workshop materials.

Location and venue

The conference will take place in Almaty, Kazakhstan at Mechanics and Mathematics Faculty, the campus of Al-Farabi Kazakh National University. More details and maps

Contacts

The programme chair is academician Yuri L. Ershov.

The chair of the Organizing Committee is Serikzhan Badaev.

Enquiries on scientific matters should be sent to Timur Bakibayev or Kuanysh Abeshev.
