Workshop on Computability Problems

August 2 - 9, 2018, Almaty, Kazakhstan

The Workshop on Computability Problems is organized and hosted by Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics at Al-Farabi Kazakh National University. It will be held at the Al-Farabi Kazakh National University from August 2 to August 9, 2018.

The program of the Workshop consists of 50-minutes plenary talks and research in groups. There are 9 invited speakers, researchers from Kazakhstan, and students.

Submissions

Abstracts of plenary talks must be submitted as tex-files.

The deadline for abstract submission is July 15, 2018. Files with problems may be submitted during the workshop.

Location and venue

The conference will take place in Almaty, Kazakhstan at Mechanics and Mathematics Faculty, the main campus of al-Farabi Kazakh National University. More details and maps

Programme Chair

The programme chair is an Academician of Russian Academy of Sciences, Director of Sobolev Institute of Mathematics Yuri L. Ershov.

Organizing Committee

The chair of the Organizing committee is Serikzhan Badaev.

Enquiries on scientific matters should be sent to Timur Bakibayev (timurbakibayev@gmail.com) or Kuanysh Abeshev (kuanqk@gmail.com).
